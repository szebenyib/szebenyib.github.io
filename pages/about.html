1. About this blog

This is a blog about my perceptions I form during my work as a SAP consultant. I intend to bring two perspectives into my articles here:

  • management perspective
  • technical perspective

The aim of management perspective articles is not to go into technical details, but to understand the implications of using a certain technology. Articles falling into this category are more interesting for project managers and decision makers.

Technical perspective on the other hand focuses on the nuts and bolts of interesting topics I have come across. Such posts may be of interest to those who employ the technology themselves.

2. About me

I am a consultant working with SAP focusing on the technical aspect in my job. My main area of expertise is Fiori, S/4HANA embedded analytics (EA) and ABAP development.

I like to go one step further than just using the technology in a 'copy-paste' or 'that's how we do it' manner and always try to understand how a certain functionality works to make the most out of it. Learning best practices takes time but it helps to find optimal solutions for customers' needs. Reading blog articles, books, taking openSAP courses and not forgetting to check the ABAP documentation is the foundation of my knowledge.

As for my personal life I am a pilot, I like mountain biking, hiking and tinkering with IT and electronics stuff.
