1. About this blog

This is a blog about my perceptions I form during my work as a SAP consultant. I intend to bring two perspectives into my articles here:

  • management perspective
  • technical perspective

The aim of management perspective articles is not to go into technical details, but to understand the implications of using a certain technology. Articles falling into this category are more interesting for project managers and decision makers.

Technical perspective on the other hand focuses on the nuts and bolts of interesting topics I have come across. Such posts may be of interest to those who employ the technology themselves.

2. About me

I am a consultant working with SAP focusing on the technical aspect in my job. My main area of expertise is Fiori, S/4HANA embedded analytics (EA) and ABAP development.

I like to go one step further than just using the technology in a 'copy-paste' or 'that's how we do it' manner and always try to understand how a certain functionality works to make the most out of it. Learning best practices takes time but it helps to find optimal solutions for customers' needs. Reading blog articles, books, taking openSAP courses and not forgetting to check the ABAP documentation is the foundation of my knowledge.

As for my personal life I am a pilot, I like mountain biking, hiking and tinkering with IT and electronics stuff.

2.1. Detailed work experience, projects

The below listing contains the clients and summary of projects that I have worked on as an external consultant. As I have been working for a consulting firm this does not mean that I have hopped from company to company but accomplished these at the same employer.

2.1.1. 2020-2022 BPW-HUNGARIA KFT. (Fiori, Embedded Analytics, ABAP)

The project being a greenfield S/4HANA (2020) implementation I was assigned the task to handle all Fiori related issues. Be it the partly basis related Fiori frontend installation and configuration or the numeruous issues arising from incorrectly working tiles or the analysis of backend BOPF logic or extending CDS views, I had the chance to meet them all. Besides all things Fiori I also implemented an interface to/from Maximo consisting of multiple SOAP web services, which offered me an opportunity to practice the new syntax, along with ABAP OO and ABAP Unit. I have also developed reports based on CDS technology with different outputs: Analytic Query, ALV with IDA and Fiori List Page. Other developments include an Excel based up- and downloader, an iDOC sender program towards the Pool4Tool system, an asset report based on a logical database and a POWL based freight unit mass editor tool.

2.1.2. 2021 FGSZ ZRT. (Fiori, ABAP)

Conducted a multi day Fiori workshop to introduce the technical background of Fiori applications covering both the UI and the gateway side. I have implemented three one-screen applications to enable mobile consumption of previously desktop-only WebDynpro HR applications.

2.1.3. 2020-2021 LESAFFRE MAGYARORSZÁG ÉLESZTŐGYÁRTÓ ÉS KERESKEDELMI KFT. (Fiori)

Taught the Hungarian SAP CC team the basics of Fiori so that in house support may be provided for their existing solutions. Later on I was tasked with the front end side of the development of a material maintenance application that enabled non-SAP subsidiaries an easy and straightforward access to view and update just the necessary aspects of materials. The solution was realized using Fiori Elements.

2.1.4. 2019 MOL PLC. (BW, BPC, ABAP, Excel-VBA)

Working as a member of the team that consisted of Hungary’s most experienced and well-know consultants in the area of SAP business planning who were tasked with putting the business planning of MOL GROUP onto SAP BPC was a true honor. Being one of the biggest and most complex firms in Hungary the task was quite a challenge. Besides being responsible for a business unit I have also contributed to our common goal by implementing automated Excel uploading of data to the system via Analysis Office, coding an overwhelming amount of FOX and ABAP code to care for every possible need of the customer.

2.1.5. 2018 MVMI ZRT. (Fiori, ABAP)

Launching customized standard Fiori applications to bring mobile experience to SRM users. The task included fixing the standard logic in a non-distruptive way without touching the standard code and relying on extensions. The biggest challenge was bringing the necessary information from the backend system to the user interface and then transforming a single click into an approval or denial for multiple types SRM objects – for which the MyInbox application was a framework only but far from being an out-of-the-box solution.

2.1.6. 2018 AUDI HUNGARIA ZRT. (Fiori)

Implementing a custom designed electronic goods receipt solution in accordance with SAP Fiori standards. The developed application supports mobile devices running 24/7 in an environment with no stable signal coverage. I have created the application frontend from the mockup phase to the implementation, the latter of which was accomplished via a behaviour/test driven development heavily using one page acceptance tests.

2.1.7. 2017 AUDI HUNGARIA ZRT. (Fiori)
