1. Profile

SAP consultant with 8 years of experience in SAP BW and SAP Fiori. At the beginning of this period I got the opportunity to familiarize myself with SAP BW, I have participated in a greenfield BW reporting implementation, which has been later extended with BW-Integrated Planning into a business planning solution. Later I have been tasked with larger and larger one man projects, including crafting ABAP logic in the source and BW systems as well as Analyzer reports and Web Templates. I have not only experienced one man show projects but ones of a higher scale as well, a prominent example being the transition of a national oil company from Excel to SAP BPC (integrated). I have also taken part in implementing a self-service solution tailored to the needs of utility companies, known as SAP Multichannel Foundation for Utilities. As it used SAPUI5 for frontend technology, this has started me on my journey with SAPUI5/Fiori that I have put to good use in numerous other projects. As I had reporting experience I have also familiarized myself with CDS views to deliver reports besides getting acquainted with other S4 related technologies in a multi-year implementation project.

2. Projects

Be it the partly basis related Fiori frontend installation and configuration or the numeruous issues arising from incorrectly working tiles or the analysis of backend BOPF logic or extending CDS views, I had the chance to meet them all. I have also developed reports based on CDS technology with different outputs: Analytic Query, ALV with IDA and Fiori List Page. Other developments include an Excel based up- and downloader, an iDOC sender program towards the Pool4Tool system, an asset report based on a logical database and a POWL based freight unit mass editor tool.
